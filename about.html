About

Hello, my name is Barry Ervin. I am a Web Developer and visual artist based in Atlanta, GA. I have worked in the film industry for the past three years. I worked on the HBO TV shows Watchmen and Lovecraft Country. In addition I worked on the indie feature Her Smell starring Elisabeth Moss. I also produce and direct music videos and short films. I am excited to learn full stack web developement so that I can apply my varied skills and interests on a day-to-day basis in my job.
